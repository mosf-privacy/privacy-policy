Quick Help

  • iCloud Sync: Ensure you’re signed in to iCloud and iCloud Drive is enabled. Changes may take a moment to sync.
  • Marked items: Items with !m appear in the “Marked” section.
  • Command Pad: Swipe right in a category to open the Command Pad. Edit the list as text, then tap Apply.
  • Delete: Swipe left on a category or item to delete (with confirmation).
